1. "Atomic Habits" by James Clear

A powerful book that explores the science of habit formation and provides practical strategies for building and breaking habits to achieve personal and professional success.

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear Full Audiobook

2. "Becoming" by Michelle Obama

In this inspiring memoir, Michelle Obama shares her journey from the South Side of Chicago to becoming the First Lady of the United States, offering valuable insights and lessons along the way.

"Becoming" by Michelle Obama Full Audiobook

3. "Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind" by Yuval Noah Harari

Delve into the history of our species with this thought-provoking book that explores the development of Homo sapiens and our impact on the world.

"Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind" by Yuval Noah Harari Full Audiobook

4. "The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho

A captivating allegorical novel that follows the journey of a young shepherd as he seeks his personal legend, imparting timeless wisdom about following one's dreams.

"The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho Full Audiobook

5. "Educated" by Tara Westover

This memoir tells the powerful story of Tara Westover, who grew up in a strict and isolated environment in rural Idaho and defied the odds to pursue education and liberation.

"Educated" by Tara Westover Full Audiobook

6. "The Power of Now" by Eckhart Tolle

An influential spiritual guide, this book teaches readers to embrace the present moment, find inner peace, and live a more fulfilling life by letting go of past regrets and future anxieties.

"The Power of Now" by Eckhart Tolle Full Audiobook

7. "Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion" by Robert Cialdini

Discover the principles of persuasion and learn how to navigate the complex world of influence and decision-making with this fascinating book.

"Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion" by Robert Cialdini Full Audiobook
